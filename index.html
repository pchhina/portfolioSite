About

I just like to play with data. The open-source tools available today makes it hard not to have fun with data. The community developing these tools is hard not to be a part of! Broad topics I am interested in for data exploration purposes are technology, climate change, energy, transportation and public health.

The tools I currently use are vim and R. I have recently started to get my hands dirty with a bit of Python too. Feel free to checkout my projects and posts. I try to make all the code available on Github. You are welcome to contact me if you think we can collaborate on a project.
